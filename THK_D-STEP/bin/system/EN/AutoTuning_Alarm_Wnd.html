1.Please use this system when POWER OFF button is valid.
 When data communication error occurred,the Actuator will not stop and this may result the damage to the work. In auto tuning, You cannot operate other dialogs and vibrations may occur. When abnormal movement occurred, please stop the Actuator quickly.

2.Please keep away the Actuator movement area and Do not touch the movable parts in tuning.
 Gain adjustment will make the reciprocate motion by the step data parameters.

3.Please confirm the normal operation of the Actuator.
 If Actuator have not normal operation, the automatic adjustment also could not be run.

4.Please confirm the operating conditions of the Actuator.
 If there is an error condition of the action condition,There may be a conflict in the movable part,There may cause the damage to the product.In addition, please set the safety stop device in the machine.Please do origin reset action in advance, Confirm action direction.

5.If the load inertia ratio is not implemented, please confirm the setting parameters.
 If wrong parameters of Load Inertia Ratio is setted,Auto Tuning will not move normally.

6.After the auto tuning,please make the test run.
 Auto tuning parameters may cause vibrations and over shock. Please adjust the gain at this time,The result of the adjustment is the reference value, not the compensation value.
